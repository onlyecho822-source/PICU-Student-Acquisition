## THE STRATEGY IN ONE SENTENCE

Every recipient is a walking testimonial AND a referral source.
They experienced the ceremony. They felt the recognition.
They know 10 people who deserve the same thing.

The outreach has two moves:
1. CELEBRATE — acknowledge what they received, invite them to share it
2. ASK — "Who in your world deserves this recognition too?"

---

## THE OUTREACH SEQUENCE (3 touches)

### TOUCH 1 — Celebration Message (Send First)
No ask. Pure recognition. Remind them of what they carry now.

### TOUCH 2 — Share Your Story (3 days later)
Ask them to post about their experience.
Give them exact words to make it easy.

### TOUCH 3 — The Friend Ask (7 days after Touch 1)
"Who else in your world has done this level of work?"
This is where the referral pipeline opens.
