The Challenge

Most Ministry Leaders Want a Doctorate But Face Major Barriers

78% of pastors express interest in advanced education, yet fewer than 15% pursue it.
  • High Cost: Traditional doctoral programs cost $15,000 to $50,000 or more.
  • Rigid Schedules: Often require relocation or significant time away from ministry.
  • Life Demands: Family and congregation responsibilities make full-time study impossible.
  • Unclear Path: Many feel called to grow but see no realistic path forward.
