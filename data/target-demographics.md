# Target Demographics
## Ideal Doctoral Candidate Profiles

---

## Executive Summary

This document defines the target demographics for PICU doctoral program recruitment. The ideal candidate is a working professional aged 30-55 seeking flexible, affordable, faith-based doctoral education. Five primary personas represent the core target segments.

## Persona Profiles

### Persona 1: Pastor Paul

**Demographics:**
- Age: 45
- Location: Suburban Texas
- Income: $65,000
- Family: Married, 3 children

**Background:**
Pastor Paul has led a mid-sized church for 12 years. He holds a Bachelor's in Theology and has extensive practical ministry experience but lacks formal doctoral credentials. His denomination increasingly expects senior pastors to hold advanced degrees.

**Goals:**
- Earn doctorate to maintain credibility
- Deepen theological knowledge
- Qualify for denominational leadership roles

**Challenges:**
- Limited time between pastoral duties
- Cannot relocate for residency programs
- Budget constraints on church salary

**PICU Fit:**
- Doctor of Divinity in Chaplaincy and Pastoral Care
- 100% online fits pastoral schedule
- $6,400 tuition is affordable

**Where to Find:**
- Pastor conferences
- Denominational networks
- Christian leadership LinkedIn groups
- Church leadership podcasts
