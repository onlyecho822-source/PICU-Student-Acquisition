## Creative Best Practices

### Image Guidelines
- Use authentic, diverse imagery
- Include people when possible
- Minimal text on images (<20%)
- High resolution (1200x628 for feed)

### Video Guidelines
- Capture attention in first 3 seconds
- Design for sound-off viewing
- Include captions
- 15-30 seconds optimal length

### Copy Guidelines
- Lead with benefit
- Include social proof
- Clear call-to-action
- Address objections
