## PRESS RELEASE DRAFT

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**POINSETTE INTERNATIONAL CHRISTIAN UNIVERSITY ANNOUNCES
FALL 2026 HONORARY DOCTORATE CEREMONY IN ATLANTA, GEORGIA**

*Nominations Now Open for Kingdom Leaders Whose Life's Work
Deserves Formal Recognition*

FISHERS, INDIANA — Poinsette International Christian University
(PICU), under the leadership of Chancellor Dr. Lesley Poinsette,
has announced its Fall 2026 Honorary Doctorate Ceremony,
to be held in Atlanta, Georgia.

The announcement follows a landmark Spring 2026 ceremony in
Indianapolis, Indiana, where seven extraordinary leaders received
Honorary Doctoral degrees recognizing decades of transformational
work in ministry, counseling, humanitarianism, and Kingdom
entrepreneurship.

"Atlanta is a city full of people who have done doctoral-level
Kingdom work without ever being formally recognized for it,"
said Dr. Lesley Poinsette, Chancellor. "We are bringing PICU's
Honorary Doctorate program to Atlanta because the leaders there
deserve to be seen."

Nominations for the Fall 2026 Atlanta ceremony are now open.
PICU awards Honorary Doctorates across six categories:
Ministry & Theology, Christian Counseling, Christian Entrepreneurship,
Christian Leadership & Business, Humanitarianism, and
Christian Ethics & Business Management.

The Honorary Doctorate includes full academic regalia,
an official PICU diploma with university seal, formal conferment
at the ceremony, a press release distributed to local media,
and permanent recognition in PICU's Hall of Honor.

To nominate yourself or someone deserving of this recognition,
visit poinsettechristianuniversity.com or contact PICU directly
at info@poinsettechristianuniversity.com or 1-877-712-PICU.

**About Poinsette International Christian University**
Poinsette International Christian University is an online
institution dedicated to providing faith-based education with
academic excellence. Founded by Dr. Lesley Poinsette, PICU's
Honorary Doctorate program recognizes leaders whose service
and Kingdom impact have gone unrecognized. Learn more at
poinsettechristianuniversity.com.
