Poinsette International Christian University is a fully accredited private Christian university offering 100% online degree programs. ### Core Values

The university operates on the principle that academic aspirations and spiritual journey should come together to shape a brighter future. PICU emphasizes excellence, faith, and service in all its programs.

## Academic Programs

### Doctoral Programs

PICU offers several doctoral-level programs designed for working professionals seeking advanced credentials in ministry, counseling, and entrepreneurship.

#### Doctor of Divinity (D.Div.) in Chaplaincy and Pastoral Care
**Tuition:** $6,400.00 | **Application Fee:** $95.00

This degree program prepares students to serve in parishes, dioceses, or other ministerial settings with in-depth theological knowledge and effective pastoral skills. Graduates are equipped for leadership roles in various chaplaincy environments.

#### Doctor of Philosophy in Christian Entrepreneurship
**Tuition:** $6,400.00 | **Application Fee:** $95.00

This program equips students to further explore the depths of entrepreneurship in the global marketplace and leadership dynamics. It integrates faith principles with business acumen for the modern Christian entrepreneur.

#### Doctor of Philosophy in Practical Ministry
**Tuition:** $6,400.00 | **Application Fee:** $95.00

Scholars in this advanced degree program are prepared with knowledge in the history and context of the Bible for assessing the beliefs and actions of Christians. The program develops practical ministry skills alongside theological depth.

### Hybrid Dual Degree Programs

#### MA in Christian Counseling & PhD in Christian Leadership & Business
**Tuition:** $7,500.00 | **Application Fee:** $95.00

These dual degrees provide students with further academic study with a biblical view in counseling and business principles in the marketplace. Graduates emerge with credentials in both counseling and business leadership.

#### M.Sc. in Practical Ministry & PhD in Christian Counseling
**Tuition:** $7,500.00 | **Application Fee:** $95.00

These dual degrees provide students with advanced study in biblical psychology, in-depth knowledge of scripture, and selected psychotherapies. The combination prepares graduates for comprehensive ministry and counseling roles.

#### MA in Theology (Divinity) & PhD in Practical Ministry
**Tuition:** $7,500.00 | **Application Fee:** $95.00

These dual degrees provide students with further academic study in history and context of the Bible for assessing the beliefs and actions of Christians. This combination offers both theological depth and practical ministry application.

### Master's Programs

### Bachelor's Programs
