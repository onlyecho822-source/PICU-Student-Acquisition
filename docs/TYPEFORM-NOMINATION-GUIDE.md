# TYPEFORM NOMINATION FORM BUILD GUIDE

**Objective:** Build a professional, high-converting nomination form for the PICU Honorary Doctorate that can be embedded on wepicu.live.
**Time Required:** 20 minutes
**Technical Skill Required:** None (Drag and drop only)

Typeform is recommended because it presents one question at a time, which feels like a conversation rather than a legal document. This dramatically increases the completion rate for nominations.

---

## STEP 1: ACCOUNT SETUP (2 Minutes)
1. Go to [Typeform.com](https://www.typeform.com/)
2. Click **"Sign up"** (The free plan is sufficient to start).
3. Create an account using your email or Google account.
4. When asked what you are using Typeform for, select "Lead Generation" or "Other".

## STEP 2: CREATE THE FORM (3 Minutes)
1. On your workspace dashboard, click the black **"Create typeform"** button.
2. Select **"Start from scratch"**.
3. Name it: **"PICU Honorary Doctorate Nomination"**.
4. Click **"Continue"**.

## STEP 3: ADD THE QUESTIONS (10 Minutes)
You will now add questions one by one using the left-hand menu. Click the **"+"** button to add a new question type.

**Question 1: Welcome Screen**
*   **Type:** Welcome Screen
*   **Text:** "Nominate a Servant Leader for the PICU Honorary Doctorate."
*   **Description:** "We recognize those who have done the work but have never been formally recognized. Tell us about someone who deserves this honor."
*   **Button Text:** "Start Nomination"

**Question 2: Nominator's Name**
*   **Type:** Short Text
*   **Text:** "First, what is your name?"
*   **Settings:** Required (Toggle the switch to ON)

**Question 3: Nominator's Email**
*   **Type:** Email
*   **Text:** "What is your email address? (We will only use this to contact you about this nomination)."
*   **Settings:** Required

**Question 4: Nominee's Name**
*   **Type:** Short Text
*   **Text:** "Who are you nominating for the Honorary Doctorate?"
*   **Settings:** Required

**Question 5: Nominee's Contact Info**
*   **Type:** Short Text
*   **Text:** "What is the best way to contact them? (Email, phone number, or social media link)"
*   **Settings:** Required

**Question 6: The Work**
*   **Type:** Long Text
*   **Text:** "Tell us about their work. Why do they deserve this recognition?"
*   **Description:** "Focus on their community impact, years of service, and the lives they have touched."
*   **Settings:** Required

**Question 7: Social Proof (Optional)**
*   **Type:** Website
*   **Text:** "Do they have a website, ministry page, or social media profile we can look at?"
*   **Settings:** Not required

**Question 8: Ending Screen**
*   **Type:** Ending Screen
*   **Text:** "Thank you for your nomination. We see their work. Our committee will review this submission and reach out shortly."

## STEP 4: DESIGN & BRANDING (3 Minutes)
1. Click the **"Design"** tab (the teardrop icon on the right menu).
2. Choose a clean, professional theme (e.g., black text on white background, or a subtle gold/blue accent to match PICU branding).
3. Keep it simple. The focus should be on the questions.

## STEP 5: PUBLISH & EMBED (2 Minutes)
1. Click the black **"Publish"** button in the top right corner.
2. Click the **"Share"** tab at the top of the screen.
3. **To get the link:** Copy the direct link provided. You can paste this into DMs, emails, and social media posts.
4. **To embed on wepicu.live:** Click on **"Embed in a web page"** on the left menu. Choose "Standard" or "Full page", click "Start embedding", and copy the provided code. Paste this code into your website builder.

---

## HOW TO MANAGE NOMINATIONS
When someone fills out the form, you will receive an email notification.
You can view all submissions by logging into Typeform and clicking the **"Results"** tab at the top of your form editor.
