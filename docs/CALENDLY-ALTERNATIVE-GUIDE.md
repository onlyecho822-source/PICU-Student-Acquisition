# FASTEST FREE CALENDLY ALTERNATIVE: CAL.COM

**Objective:** Set up a professional booking link for PICU Honorary Degree candidate calls without paying monthly fees or entering a credit card.

**The Recommendation:** **Cal.com**
Cal.com is the best free alternative to Calendly in 2026. Unlike Calendly's free tier which limits you to one event type, Cal.com gives individuals unlimited event types and unlimited bookings completely free, forever. No credit card is required to sign up.

---

## STEP-BY-STEP SETUP GUIDE (10 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Free Account
1. Go to [Cal.com](https://cal.com)
2. Click the black **"Get started"** or **"Sign up with Google"** button.
3. If you use Google Workspace/Gmail for Dr. Poinsette, choose "Sign up with Google" for instant calendar connection.
4. Choose your username (e.g., `cal.com/drpoinsette` or `cal.com/picu`).

### Step 2: Connect Your Calendar
1. During onboarding, it will ask to connect your calendar.
2. Authorize it to read your Google Calendar or Outlook Calendar.
3. *Why this matters:* This ensures candidates can only book when you are actually free, preventing double-booking.

### Step 3: Create the PICU Event Type
1. On your dashboard, click **"Event Types"** on the left menu.
2. Click **"+ New"** to create a new event.
3. **Title:** "15-Minute PICU Conversation"
4. **URL:** `15min` (so your link becomes `cal.com/drpoinsette/15min`)
5. **Description:** "A brief, confidential conversation about your nomination for the PICU Honorary Doctorate."
6. **Duration:** 15 minutes.

### Step 4: Set Your Availability
1. Click on **"Availability"** in the left menu.
2. Set the hours you are willing to take these calls (e.g., Tuesdays and Thursdays, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM).
3. Cal.com will automatically translate these times into the candidate's local time zone when they click your link.

### Step 5: Copy and Paste Your Link
1. Go back to **"Event Types"**.
2. Click the **"Copy Link"** button next to your "15-Minute PICU Conversation" event.
3. Paste this link into all of your outreach templates in `outreach/HONORARY-DEGREE-TEMPLATES.md` and `outreach/MESSAGE-TEMPLATES.md`.
