# NOMINATION FORM — BUILD GUIDE
**Platform:** Google Forms (fastest, free, no account needed beyond Google)
**Setup time:** 18 minutes
**Updated:** 2026-03-29T23:12:49Z

---

## WHY GOOGLE FORMS

Dr. Poinsette likely already has a Google account.
Google Forms is free, unlimited responses, embeds on any website,
and sends email notifications automatically.
No new account needed.

---

## STEP-BY-STEP BUILD (18 minutes)

### Step 1 — Open Google Forms (1 min)
Go to forms.google.com
Click the "+" button to create a new form.

### Step 2 — Set title and description (2 min)
Title: **Nominate Someone for a PICU Honorary Doctorate**

Description:
"Do you know someone whose life's work deserves formal recognition?
Someone who has given decades to ministry, community service,
or Kingdom leadership — and has never been honored for it?
PICU was built for them. Dr. Poinsette personally reviews every nomination."

### Step 3 — Add Section 1: About You (3 min)
Question 1: "Your Full Name" — Short answer — Required
Question 2: "Your Email" — Short answer — Required
Question 3: "Your relationship to the nominee" — Dropdown — Required
Options: I am nominating myself / Friend or colleague / Church member /
         Family member / Fellow minister / Other

### Step 4 — Add Section 2: Who Are You Nominating? (3 min)
Question 4: "Nominee Full Name" — Short answer — Required
Question 5: "Nominee Email (optional)" — Short answer — Not required
Question 6: "Nominee City / State / Country" — Short answer — Required

### Step 5 — Add Section 3: Their Work (5 min)
Question 7: "What does this person do? (ministry, business, community service)"
Paragraph — Required — Hint: "3 sentences is enough"

Question 8: "How long have they been doing this work?"
Dropdown — Required
Options: 5-10 years / 10-15 years / 15-20 years / 20-30 years / 30+ years

Question 9: "Why do they deserve this recognition?"
Paragraph — Required

### Step 6 — Add Section 4: Degree Category (2 min)
Question 10: "Which honorary degree fits their work best?"
Dropdown — Required
Options:
- Ministry & Theology
- Christian Counseling
- Christian Leadership & Business
- Christian Ethics & Business Management
- Humanitarianism
- Christian Entrepreneurship
- Not sure — let Dr. Poinsette decide

### Step 7 — Confirmation message (1 min)
Click Settings → Presentation → Confirmation message:
"Thank you for your nomination. Dr. Poinsette personally reviews
every submission. You will hear back within 5 business days.
Learn more at wepicu.live."

### Step 8 — Set up email notifications (1 min)
Click the 3-dot menu → Get email notifications for new responses.
Enter Dr. Poinsette's email. Every nomination sends her an email immediately.

### Step 9 — Get your form link (0 min)
Click Send → Copy the link.
That is your public nomination URL.

## ADD THE LINK TO THESE PLACES

Once you have the form link:
1. Add to PICU website (poinsettechristianuniversity.com)

## EMBED ON WEBSITE

Google Forms gives you an embed code:
Click Send → Embed (< >) → Copy the iframe code
Paste into your website HTML where you want the form.
