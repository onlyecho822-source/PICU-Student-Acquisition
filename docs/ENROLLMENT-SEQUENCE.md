## THE DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT

**Opening (Dr. Poinsette):**
"[Name], thank you so much for making time.
I have been following what you have built and I just
want to hear it from you — tell me about your work.
What are you most proud of?"

**[Let them talk for 5 minutes. Listen. Take notes.]**

**The Bridge:**
"What you just described — that is exactly why I called.
At PICU we created the Honorary Doctorate program because
I kept seeing leaders like you doing doctoral-level Kingdom work
with no formal recognition. Lady J is a perfect example —
17 years of counseling and mentoring, survived everything you can
imagine, and nobody had ever put a hood on her shoulders and said
'we see what you did.' We did that on March 28th.
I want to do the same for you."

**The Offer:**
"I would like to formally nominate you for a PICU Honorary Doctorate.
Can I send you the acceptance letter today?"

**If they ask about cost:**
"The full investment is $2,500 — that covers your regalia,
your diploma, the ceremony, a press release to your local media,
and permanent recognition in our Hall of Honor.
For context, a traditional doctoral program costs $30,000 to $60,000
and takes years. This is PICU formally recognizing the doctorate
you have already earned through your life's work.
We ask for a $500 deposit to reserve your seat —
that is the only non-refundable portion.
We also have payment plans if that is helpful."

**The Close:**
"Can I send the acceptance letter today?
I want your name in the next class."
