## THE PROBLEM WITH THE OLD SEQUENCE

Old: Outreach → Acceptance letter → $500 deposit
Issue: Gap between "we want to honor you" and "$500" was too wide.
People hesitated. No emotional bridge.

## THE NEW SEQUENCE

```
STEP 1  Outreach message (DM/email/referral intro)
           ↓
STEP 2  15-minute call with Dr. Poinsette personally
        (This is the bridge. This is where the yes happens.)
           ↓
STEP 3  Formal acceptance letter sent within 24 hours of call
           ↓
STEP 4  $500 non-refundable deposit (14 days to pay)
           ↓
STEP 5  Regalia sizing + ceremony prep
           ↓
STEP 6  $2,000 balance due 30 days before ceremony
           ↓
STEP 7  CEREMONY — robed, honored, celebrated
           ↓
STEP 8  Referral ask — "Who do you want to honor next?"
```

## THE 15-MINUTE CALL SCRIPT

**Purpose:** Close the emotional gap. Make it personal.
**Who runs it:** Dr. Poinsette herself. Always.
**Length:** 15 minutes maximum.
**Calendly link:** [DR. POINSETTE'S BOOKING LINK]

### Opening (2 minutes)
"[Name], thank you for making time. I wanted to reach out
personally because [SPECIFIC REASON — e.g., Lady J told me
about your work / I've been following what you've built].

I don't do these calls with everyone.
I do them with people whose work genuinely moves me."

### The recognition (3 minutes)
Ask them to tell you about their work.
Let them talk. Listen.
Then reflect it back specifically:
"So for [X years] you have been [what they do].
And in that time [what they built / who they served]."

This is the moment they feel seen.
Don't rush it.

### The offer (3 minutes)
"What PICU does is make that official.
Lady J received her honorary doctorate from us last week —
17 years of counseling and mentoring work, formally recognized.
That is what I want to do for you.

This is not an academic program. You don't take classes.
This is PICU saying: your life's work IS the credential.
We put a diploma and a hood on it."

### The investment (2 minutes)
Only introduce pricing after the emotional yes.
Wait for them to say "I'm interested" or ask "what does this involve."

"The full investment is $2,500.
That covers your regalia, diploma, ceremony, press release,
and permanent Hall of Honor listing.
We ask for a $500 deposit to reserve your seat —
that's the only non-refundable portion.
The balance of $2,000 is due 30 days before the ceremony.
And we have payment plans if that helps."

### The close (3 minutes)
"I'm going to send you Lady J's announcement today
so you can see exactly what this looks like.
And I'll follow that with a formal acceptance letter.

All you need to do to say yes is reply to that letter
and make the deposit. That's it.

Do you have any questions for me right now?"

### After the call (2 minutes)
Send Lady J's announcement link immediately.
Follow with formal acceptance letter within 24 hours.
