The referrals become the next class of honorees.
The cycle continues.

## THE REFERRAL PROGRAM STRUCTURE

### How It Works

```
STEP 1: Dr. Poinsette personally contacts each honoree
        "You were honored because of your life's work.
         Who in YOUR world has done the same?"

STEP 2: Honoree nominates 2-3 people from their network
        Nomination form (simple — name, ministry, years of service)

STEP 3: Dr. Poinsette reviews nominations
        Approves fit based on PICU criteria

STEP 4: Honoree introduces the nominee to PICU
        "My friend Dr. Poinsette honored me —
         she wants to honor you too"

STEP 5: Nominee enrolls
        Honoree gets recognition for the referral

STEP 6: New honoree becomes a nominator in the next cycle
```

### The Referral Incentive

What does the referring honoree receive?

**Option A — Legacy Recognition**
Referring honoree is listed as the "Nominating Fellow"
on the new honoree's official PICU credentials.
"Nominated by Dr. Jacquie Murrell"
This ties their legacies together permanently.

**Option B — Scholarship in Their Name**
(Already done for Lady J — 2 scholarships in her name)
If a honoree refers 3+ enrollees, PICU establishes
a scholarship or award in their name.

**Option C — Fall 2026 Ceremony Recognition**
Honorees who refer new candidates are recognized
at the Fall 2026 Atlanta ceremony as "PICU Champions."
Public acknowledgment of their role in expanding the mission.
