# Competitive Analysis
## Market Positioning for PICU Doctoral Programs

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines the competitive landscape for online Christian doctoral programs. PICU holds a significant competitive advantage in pricing while maintaining accreditation and program quality. The primary opportunity lies in capturing price-sensitive candidates who seek legitimate credentials without the premium cost of larger institutions.

## Competitive Response Playbook

### Scenario: Competitor Price Reduction

**Response:**
- Emphasize total value (price + flexibility + speed)
- Highlight unique program features
- Offer limited-time scholarship incentives

### Scenario: New Competitor Entry

**Response:**
- Strengthen brand positioning
- Increase testimonial marketing
- Deepen partnership relationships

### Scenario: Accreditation Questions

**Response:**
- Provide clear CCACI documentation
- Share employer acceptance data
- Feature alumni career success stories
