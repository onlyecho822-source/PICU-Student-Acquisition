# Weekly Automation Tasks
## Recurring Lead Generation Activities

---

## Overview

This document outlines weekly tasks required to maintain consistent lead generation for PICU doctoral programs. Tasks are organized by day and estimated time commitment.

---

## Monday: Social Media Monitoring

### LinkedIn (45 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Group monitoring | Check 5 target groups for degree-seeking posts | 15 min |
| Engagement | Comment on 3-5 relevant discussions | 15 min |
| Connection requests | Send 10 personalized connection requests | 10 min |
| Content sharing | Share 1 educational post | 5 min |

### Facebook (30 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Group monitoring | Check 5 target groups for questions | 15 min |
| Engagement | Provide helpful responses to 2-3 posts | 15 min |

### Reddit (15 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Subreddit check | Review r/PhD, r/theology, r/GradSchool | 10 min |
| Engagement | Provide 1-2 helpful comments | 5 min |

---

## Tuesday: Forum Engagement

### DegreeInfo/DegreeForum (30 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| New thread review | Check for new doctoral program discussions | 10 min |
| Responses | Provide helpful answers to 2-3 questions | 15 min |
| Thread monitoring | Check responses to previous posts | 5 min |

### GradCafe (15 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Theology section | Review new discussions | 10 min |
| Engagement | Provide 1 helpful response | 5 min |

---

## Wednesday: Content Creation

### Blog/Article (60 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Topic selection | Choose keyword-targeted topic | 10 min |
| Research | Gather information and sources | 15 min |
| Writing | Draft 800-1,200 word article | 30 min |
| Optimization | Add keywords, links, meta data | 5 min |

### Social Media Content (30 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Content calendar | Plan week's social posts | 10 min |
| Graphics | Create 2-3 social media graphics | 15 min |
| Scheduling | Schedule posts for the week | 5 min |

---

## Thursday: Lead Follow-Up

### Email Outreach (45 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| New leads | Send personalized follow-up to new inquiries | 20 min |
| Nurture sequence | Review and update email sequences | 15 min |
| Re-engagement | Send re-engagement emails to cold leads | 10 min |

### Phone Follow-Up (30 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Hot leads | Call leads who requested information | 20 min |
| Voicemails | Leave personalized voicemails | 10 min |

---

## Friday: Analytics and Optimization

### Performance Review (30 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Website analytics | Review traffic sources and behavior | 10 min |
| Ad performance | Check Google/Facebook ad metrics | 10 min |
| Email metrics | Review open rates, click rates | 5 min |
| Lead tracking | Update CRM with new leads | 5 min |

### Optimization (30 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| Ad adjustments | Pause underperforming ads, boost winners | 15 min |
| Keyword updates | Add negative keywords, adjust bids | 10 min |
| A/B testing | Set up new tests for next week | 5 min |

---

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|

| Urgent responses | Respond to time-sensitive inquiries | 15 min |

### Sunday (Optional - 15 minutes)

| Task | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
