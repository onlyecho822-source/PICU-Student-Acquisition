## Idea 1: Military Precision Meets Divine Authority

**Design Movement:** Neo-Classical with Military Formalism

**Core Principles:**
- Sharp geometric lines conveying discipline and structure
- Gold accents on deep forest green (military honor meets nature/growth)
- Symmetrical layouts suggesting order and trustworthiness
- Heavy serif typography for gravitas and permanence

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Deep Forest Green (#0D3B1F) - growth, faith, military heritage
- Accent: Antique Gold (#C9A227) - honor, achievement, prestige
- Text: Ivory (#FFFFF0) on dark, Charcoal (#2C2C2C) on light
- Highlight: Burgundy (#722F37) for CTAs

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width hero with diagonal gold stripe (military sash reference)
- Three-column tier displays with gold borders
- Centered monumental headings with generous letter-spacing

**Signature Elements:**
- Gold laurel wreath motifs around tier badges
- Diagonal stripe accents (military ribbon inspiration)
- Embossed seal/crest effect for credentials

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Deliberate, weighty transitions (300ms ease-out)
- Hover states reveal gold underlines
- Scroll-triggered fade-ups for gravitas

**Animation:**
- Slow, dignified entrance animations
- Gold shimmer effect on hover for premium feel
- Parallax on hero section

**Typography System:**
- Headlines: Playfair Display Bold (64px hero, 48px section)
- Body: Lato Regular (18px)
- Accents: Montserrat SemiBold for buttons/labels
</text>

## Selected Approach: Idea 1 - Military Precision Meets Divine Authority

This approach best represents:
- Veteran-founded heritage (military formalism)
- Faith-based mission (classical, dignified)
- High-profile prestige (gold accents, formal typography)
- Authority and trust (symmetry, structure)

The deep forest green with antique gold creates a unique, memorable brand that stands apart from typical blue/white educational sites while honoring both military service and spiritual mission.
